# **App Name**: Clinic Flow

## Core Features:

- QR Code Patient Registration: Patients scan a QR code, fill in their details, and receive a token.
- Token Generation: Generates a unique token upon patient registration.
- Queue Display: Display queue details on a TV browser, with video ads running in 70% of the display and queue details in 30%.
- Doctor Call Notification with TTS: When a doctor calls a patient, a pop-up appears on the screen with Text-to-Speech of the token number, patient name, and cabin to go to.
- Consultation Management: Doctors can start/end consultations and mark patients as no-shows. Doctors also have access to patient history and the ability to add notes. An AI tool helps them summarise the consultation.
- Assistant Token Creation: Assistants can create tokens on behalf of patients.
- Clinic Group Management: Option to create groups (Screen, Cabin, Doctor, Assistants), with each group having its own QR code and registration page. Includes a master QR code for the entire clinic, allowing patients to select their group during registration.
- Advertising Management: Central Admin portal with Advertiser and Campaign options. Advertiser users can upload videos to be played on screens and track video impression counts, clinic count, screen count, and patient count during playback.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A calm, professional blue (#007BFF) reminiscent of a dependable healthcare setting.
- Background color: Light grey (#F8F9FA) for a clean, modern, and unobtrusive backdrop.
- Accent color: A complementary teal (#20C997), contrasting with the primary blue and adds a touch of elegance.
- Body and headline font: 'Roboto', a sans-serif font, for a clean and modern look suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Use clear and professional icons to represent different functionalities, such as user profiles, clinic settings, and advertising management.
- Employ a clean and structured layout with clear sections for patient registration, queue display, and admin controls. Utilize white space to avoid clutter.
- Incorporate subtle animations for loading states and transitions to enhance user experience without being distracting.
